FAQ

Quick answers to common onboarding questions.

Do I need a warehouse to use DuraInventory?

No. You can track materials by project, by location, or both — depending on how your operation runs.

Can we import our existing catalog from Excel or Google Sheets?

Yes. You can import a CSV with required headers to populate your item catalog quickly.

How does historical pricing help estimating?

DuraInventory captures purchase and receiving history in the backend so future estimates can reflect real costs and confidence ranges instead of guesswork.

Is this only for renewables and T&D?

No. It’s built for any company running multiple projects that needs digital oversight over materials, procurement, and estimating.
